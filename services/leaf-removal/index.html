Don't Let Leaves Win

Professional Leaf Removal in Hamilton County

Hamilton County is full of mature oaks, maples, and sycamores. Beautiful in the summer. A nightmare in October and November when they dump thousands of pounds of leaves onto your property.

A thin layer of leaves will decompose and feed your soil. A thick, wet blanket of leaves will smother your grass, breed disease, and give pests a cozy place to spend the winter. Our leaf removal service gets them off your property before the damage is done.

Why It Matters

The Real Cost of Skipping Leaf Removal

Leaves are more than an eyesore. Left on your lawn through winter, they create serious problems that cost more to fix in spring than the removal would have cost in fall.

Snow mold. Wet leaves under snow are the #1 cause of snow mold in Hamilton County lawns. The fungus shows up as gray or pink patches in early spring and takes weeks to recover.

Pest shelter. Leaf litter is prime real estate for mice, voles, and ticks looking for winter cover. Tick populations especially thrive in leaf debris near the edges of your property.

Smothered turf. Grass needs sunlight even in its winter semi-dormancy. Thick leaf cover blocks that light and traps moisture against the blade surface, leaving you with weak, thin grass come spring.

Leaf removal pairs naturally with fall cleanups, fall aeration, and a winterizer fertilizer treatment for the strongest possible going-into-winter setup.
