What We Offer

Our Landscaping Services

Every property is different. We offer a full range of landscaping services that can be tailored to your needs and budget.

Plantings & Landscape Bed Renovations

New shrubs, perennials, ornamental grasses, and seasonal flowers installed in fresh or renovated beds. We select plants that thrive in Indiana's climate and match the light, soil, and maintenance level you're looking for. Learn more about plantings.

Mulch & Rock Installation

Dyed mulch, shredded hardwood, and decorative rock ground covers installed in landscape beds. Mulch retains moisture, regulates soil temperature, and keeps weeds down. We haul, spread, and edge. You just enjoy the result. Learn more about mulch & rock.

Landscape Trimming & Pruning

Keep your shrubs, hedges, and small trees shaped and healthy with regular trimming and seasonal pruning. Overgrown landscaping doesn't just look messy. It blocks airflow and invites disease. Learn more about trimming & pruning.

Paver Patios & Driveways

Custom paver installations for patios, walkways, and driveways. Pavers add curb appeal and functional outdoor living space that lasts for decades with minimal maintenance. Learn more about pavers.

Sod Installation

When seeding isn't fast enough or the area is too damaged for overseeding, sod gives you an instant lawn. We prep the soil, lay the sod, and get you growing. Learn more about sod installation.

Seasonal Cleanups

Spring and fall cleanup services to clear debris, remove leaves, edge beds, and get your property ready for the next season. Learn more about cleanups.
