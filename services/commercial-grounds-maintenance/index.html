What's Included

A Complete Program, Not a La Carte Guesswork

Every commercial property is different. We build a custom maintenance plan around your property's specific needs, but most programs include some combination of the following.

Growing Season (April through November)

Weekly mowing, edging, and blowing to keep the turf clean and professional looking. Shrub and hedge trimming on a scheduled rotation. Mulch installation in spring to refresh landscape beds. Fertilization and weed control to keep the turf thick and weed-free. Seasonal flower plantings at building entrances for color and curb appeal. Spring and fall cleanups to clear debris and prep for the next season. Leaf removal throughout autumn.

Winter (November through March)

Snow plowing and ice management for parking lots, fire lanes, loading areas, sidewalks, and building entryways. Pre-treatment before storms and de-icing after plowing. Commercial clients get priority response times, typically cleared before your business opens.

Ongoing

Mosquito control for properties with outdoor seating or gathering areas. Landscape bed weed control to keep beds clean without constant manual weeding. Irrigation monitoring (if applicable). Proactive communication about property conditions and upcoming work.
