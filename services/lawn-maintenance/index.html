What's Included

Building Your Maintenance Program

Every property is different. We build your program around what yours actually needs.

Weekly Lawn Care

The foundation of every maintenance program. Weekly mowing with edging, string trimming, and blowing from mid-April through November. Same crew, same day, every week. Your lawn always looks like it was just cut because it was.

Landscape Bed Upkeep

Beds don't maintain themselves. Regular maintenance includes trimming and pruning shrubs and hedges, pulling or treating weeds, monitoring plant health, and keeping edges crisp. We catch problems early before they become expensive fixes.

Seasonal Services

Spring cleanup to start the season clean. Fertilization and weed control through the growing months. Aeration and overseeding in fall. Fall cleanup to protect your lawn through winter. Each service happens at the right time without you having to remember or schedule anything.

Add-On Services

Mulch and rock refresh, seasonal flower plantings, mosquito control, snow removal. Whatever your property needs, we add it to the program.

Why One Company

Stop Juggling Multiple Contractors

Hiring one company for mowing, another for fertilization, another for cleanups, and hoping they all show up when they're supposed to is a headache nobody needs. With a Sprout maintenance program, one company handles everything. One phone number, one crew that knows your property, one bill.

This is especially valuable for commercial property managers who need reliable, consistent service across multiple properties without having to coordinate between vendors.
