What We Do in Fortville

Full-Service Lawn Care & Landscaping

From weekly mowing on a big lot to fall leaf removal under a canopy of hardwoods, we handle every part of your outdoor property. Here's what Fortville homeowners rely on us for most.

Lawn Mowing & Maintenance

Fortville lots tend to be larger than the typical Carmel or Fishers yard, which is exactly why you want a crew with commercial-grade equipment instead of wrestling a residential mower across a half acre every Saturday. We're on your property the same day every week from mid-April through November, with mowing, edging, string trimming, and blowing included in every visit. Learn more about our mowing service.

Fertilization & Weed Control

Fortville properties often border agricultural land and open fields, which means weed seeds blow in constantly. A strong fertilization and weed control program keeps your turf thick enough to crowd those invaders out. We apply pre-emergent treatments in spring, targeted post-emergent weed control through the season, and a winterizer application to build root strength heading into dormancy.

Aeration & Overseeding

The soil in the Fortville area compacts hard, especially on properties with heavy clay underneath. Fall core aeration opens that soil back up so water and nutrients can reach the roots, and overseeding fills in the thin spots for a thicker lawn the following spring.

Cleanups, Leaf Removal & Snow

Big mature trees mean big leaf drops. We handle fall cleanups and leaf removal so your lawn isn't smothered heading into winter. We also do spring cleanups to clear winter debris and get your beds ready for the growing season, plus snow removal for driveways and commercial lots.
