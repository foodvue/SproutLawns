What's Included

Our Basic Lawn Mowing Package

Your basic lawn mowing package includes mowing, string trimming, edging, and blowing. Our team takes care of your lawn every single week from mid-April through the first week of November.

Mowing

We mow all-sized lawns, from small residential ones to large commercial properties. Using an alternating pattern, we mow your lawn in accordance with the season. We carefully follow the one-third rule — cutting one-third of the blade length per session to ensure your grass grows at the optimal height for health.

Edging

We use a powerful edger to give your lawn a finished look. Our professionals edge around sidewalks, driveways, and landscaping beds to keep the edges between turf and hardscaping straight and neat.

String Trimming

For areas your mower can't reach, our professional-grade trimmers handle grass growing around the house, fence, trees, and hardscaping elements.

Blowing

We clean up after every session. Our team uses blowers to remove grass clippings from sidewalks and driveways, blowing them back onto the lawn. This provides extra nutrients as the clippings biodegrade.

In the Noblesville, IN region, your yard may be made up of one or a combination of cool-season turfgrasses. These grasses require different maintenance and mowing to different heights to grow strong and healthy.
