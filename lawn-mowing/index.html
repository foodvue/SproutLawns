Weekly & Bi-Weekly

Flexible Scheduling That Fits Your Life

We offer both weekly and bi-weekly mowing schedules to match your needs and budget. During Indiana's peak growing season — April through October — weekly service keeps your lawn looking its best and prevents the scalped, stressed look that comes from cutting too much at once.

Bi-weekly service is available for customers who prefer it, though we recommend weekly visits during the heaviest growth months (May and June) to avoid clumping and uneven cuts.

Your crew comes on the same day each week so you always know when to expect us. If weather pushes us back, we'll let you know and reschedule within 24 hours.
