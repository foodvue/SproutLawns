About Sprout Lawn & Landscape

Our Story

Your Neighbors, Not a National Chain

Sprout Lawn & Landscape was founded by Mike Harmon and Faith Haberlin right here in Noblesville, Indiana. What started as a small operation with a truck and a trailer has grown into a full-service lawn care and landscaping company serving seven communities across Hamilton County.

Mike is a U.S. Air Force veteran, and the way he runs Sprout reflects that background. Show up when you say you will. Do the work right the first time. And if something needs to be fixed, handle it without excuses. That's not a mission statement on a wall. It's how the crew operates every day, on every property, from Carmel to Cicero and Geist.

Faith manages the business operations that keep everything running behind the scenes, from scheduling and customer communication to making sure every property gets the attention it deserves.

Veteran Owned

Discipline, Reliability, Attention to Detail

Military service teaches you that details matter, that showing up on time isn't optional, and that the standard is the standard regardless of who's watching. Those principles translate directly to lawn care and landscaping. Every mow gets pattern rotation and sharp blades. Every fertilizer application is calibrated to the property. Every cleanup is thorough, not just a quick blow-and-go.

We're not a franchise. There's no corporate office in another state making decisions for us. When you call Sprout, you talk to the people who actually manage and perform the work. That direct accountability is something we'll never outgrow, no matter how many properties we service.
